If ICE Comes to Your Door

  • You do not have to open the door. Ask them to slide ID under the door.
  • Ask: "Do you have a warrant signed by a judge?"
  • You have the right to remain silent. Say: "I choose to remain silent."
  • Stay calm. Do not run, argue, or resist.

About This Project

The Chinatown Civic Compass is a community initiative created to empower residents with knowledge about their rights and civic engagement opportunities.

Our mission is to provide free, accessible, multilingual civic education that helps people understand their constitutional rights, especially around interactions with law enforcement and immigration authorities.

All information on this site has been reviewed by legal experts and community partners. We are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date resources for our community.
