Emergency Legal Hotlines

If you or someone you know needs immediate legal assistance, call these hotlines:

RAICES - Refugee and Immigrant Center

Free legal services for immigrant families, children, and refugees.

1-800-354-9365

National Immigrant Justice Center

Legal services, policy advocacy, and education for immigrants.

(212) 629-6335

ACLU Immigrants' Rights Project

Challenging unconstitutional laws and practices that violate immigrants' rights.

(212) 549-1200

Community Organizations

OLA of Eastern Long Island

Organizing, advocacy, and services for the Latino immigrant community on Long Island.

(631) 209-5188

Museum of Chinese in America (MOCA)

Preserving and presenting the history, heritage, culture, and experiences of people of Chinese descent in the United States.

(212) 619-9785
